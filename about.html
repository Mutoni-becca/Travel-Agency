About Rwanda Eco-Tours

Preserving Rwanda's natural heritage while providing unforgettable travel experiences

Our Story

Rwanda Eco-Tours was founded in 2015 with a mission to showcase Rwanda's incredible biodiversity while promoting sustainable tourism practices. We partner with local communities and conservation organizations to ensure that tourism benefits both visitors and Rwandans.

From the misty volcanoes of the Virunga Mountains to the serene shores of Lake Kivu, we craft journeys that connect travelers with Rwanda's soul.

Our Mission

To create meaningful travel experiences that support conservation and empower local communities.

Our Vision

A Rwanda where tourism and conservation thrive together for generations to come.
